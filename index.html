Intern Pursuit

Wonderfil

InfluxDB, Python, Pandas
Wonderfil, a company that builds smart refill stations for cosmetics products with the goal of reducing plastic waste, was storing its data on station product levels in local spreadsheets that were not compatible with its analytics dashboards, leading to hours every week being spent on formatting and transfer.

I developed a pipeline that automatically formatted and moved the time series data onto the InfluxDB cloud, cutting several hours a week of manual processing and enabling live updates of remote station data.
